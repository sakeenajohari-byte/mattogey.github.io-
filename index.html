The Story

Mattogey is a wandering soul with a guitar, telling stories picked up from back alleys, train stations, and front porches across America.

With roots deep in traditional folk and a heart set on keeping acoustic music honest, every song is a page torn from a well-worn journal — raw, unfiltered, and true.

"If a song doesn't make you feel like you're sitting around a campfire with old friends, I haven't done my job."
